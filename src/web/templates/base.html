FITEBOX

Autonomous conference recording appliance built on Raspberry Pi. Captures, composes and streams multi-source video with zero cloud dependency.

● Multi-camera compositing (HDMI + USB)
● OLED display + hardware buttons
● Live streaming to YouTube, Twitch & RTMP
● Schedule integration (Pentabarf XML)
● Web dashboard for remote control
● Ad-hoc WiFi & wired networking
OpenSouthCode

FITEBOX was born to record the talks at OpenSouthCode, a free and non-commercial conference celebrating open source software, hardware and culture in Málaga, Spain since 2016.

Held annually at La Térmica, it brings together developers, educators, artists and families around the values of transparency, collaboration and technological independence. Over 50 sessions across multiple tracks, completely free and organized by volunteers.
